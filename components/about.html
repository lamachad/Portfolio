Leandro Machado

I am a Software Engineer. I am currently focusing on the area of Full Stack Web Development with a particular interest in mobile app development using React.

Ever since I can remember I have always wanted to do something with my life that is both meaningful and challenging. This life philosophy has led me toward the path of Engineering. At school, I have always had a particular interest in both mathematics and science. My first introduction in fact in college towards computer science was when I enrolled in my first discrete mathematics course. I went from finally understanding what the term "algorithm" was, a term I had only heard of when I learned how to solve a Rubik's cube, to actually building and programming a vehicle capable of autonomously pathfinding its way out of any maze.

Right after graduating with my Bachelor's, I enrolled in a coding Bootcamp at the University of Arizona. During my six months at UA, I acquired an interest in the area of Web Development. I became a better programmer and built on the theoretical knowledge I acquired while completing my formal college education. I have experience with a variety of different tools and technologies like HandleBars.js, CSS, React, Git, MySql, Bootstrap, and jQuery. However, one of the most valuable skills that I learned during my time at UA was how to truly collaborate with a team of developers.

As a proud alumnus of both Arizona State University and the University of Arizona, I want to re-purpose everything I learned from my Introduction to Engineering to everything from Bootcamp in a way where it is not only interesting to me but to also use my skills to create life-changing technologies that truly help humanity.
